졸업선물 Grad Gift

Grad Gift is a data visualization web that summarizes reading history of graduates. "You are what you read." So much data go unnoticed when students leave school. Data such as reading history can give students an insightful time to reflect back on themselves.

Web

Grad Gift is delivered to each student with a link and a personal letter.

Collect data

The university library offers excel spreadsheets of reading data. I got familiar with the school library system so that I can refine the data later on accordingly. I also talked with the graduates to hear their stories and concerns, and thought about ways to give them a meaningful graduation gift.

"I'm not so sure of what I like. It's like I forgot what I am interested in."
"It's quite overwhelming. I am happy that I finished school but also worried because I'm not sure what I should do now."

Capture insight

As the saying goes, "You are what you read". I understood that at this point of time, may graduates undergo a phase of uncertainty. Reading data is relatively objective as it was collected for over 4 years based on student behavior. Such data can turn into information and visualization to give students a reflective moment on their interests.

You Are What You Read.

Create experience

I turned data into insights, or useful questions which I later then visualized into charts. I used python to refine raw library data and data visualization library such as matplotlib for initial data viz before desigining it for final web.
